What would happen if you didn’t drink water?- Mia Nacamulli

Introduction

TED-Ed
Url: https://youtu.be/9iMGFqMmUFs

Water content

  • Every human 55%~60%
  • Body 75% ~~like a fish
  • 1 age body 65%

    Water function

  • Lubricate joints
  • Regulate temperature
  • Nourish the brain and spinal cord

    Water in the organ

  • 3/4 water in brain&heart ~~ like a banana
  • 83% water in lungs ~~ like an apple
  • 31% water in dry human bones

    Water Loss

    Each day lose 2~3 L water

    How

  • Sweat
  • Urine
  • Bowel movement
  • Even breathing

    Low Water Level

    Happen

  • Release antidiuretic hormone
  • In the kidney, Blood will absorb and retain more water
  • Lead to dark urine, notable drops in energy, bad mood, skin moisture, blood pressure
  • Low water brain will work hard than normal >>> shrink

    Over-hydrated

    Victim

  • More >>> Athlete

    Happen

  • Slow and even stop release antidiuretic hormone
  • Sodium electrolytes will become diluted
  • Kidneys can’t keep up with the resulting volumes of dilute urine
  • Water intoxication
  • Headache
  • Vomiting
  • In rare instances, seizures or death

    Suggestion

  • Drink eight glasses a day
  • 2.5-3.7 L for men
  • 2-2.7 L for women
  • Fruits and vegetable
